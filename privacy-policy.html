Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

When you interact with the EF Moon website, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Form: Your name, email address, and any message you send when using the "Get In Touch" contact form.
  • Fan Wall: Your name/handle and any message you post to the Moonite Shoutouts wall.
  • Merch Orders: Your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and order details when purchasing official merchandise.
  • Superfans Challenge: Your name, email address, and quiz answers/score when participating in the Moonite Challenge.
  • Mailing List / SMS Alerts: Your email address and/or phone number when you sign up for newsletters or tour alerts.
  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous page views, device type, and browser information to help us improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and inquiries
  • To process and fulfill merchandise orders
  • To display approved fan messages on the Fan Wall
  • To operate and maintain the Superfans Challenge leaderboard
  • To send you news, tour dates, and music releases (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and content
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored securely on our servers (via Vercel KV or local JSON storage). We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Contact form messages, fan wall posts, and challenge results are kept until you request deletion or we remove them during routine maintenance. Order data is retained for record-keeping purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or with trusted service providers who help us operate our website (e.g., email delivery services, payment processors, and hosting providers). Your fan wall posts may be publicly displayed on the website after approval. Challenge leaderboard scores are publicly visible but only show your chosen name and score — never your email or other private details.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies for website functionality and authentication (admin login). No third-party tracking cookies are used without your consent. Our site embeds third-party content (YouTube, Spotify) which may set their own cookies — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your data (contact form submissions, fan wall posts, challenge entries, etc.) by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email. For SMS, reply "STOP" to opt out.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, data requests, or concerns, please reach out through our social channels listed on the main website, or use the "Get In Touch" contact form on the site. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
