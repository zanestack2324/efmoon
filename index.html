The Story

Who Is EF Moon?

EF Moon — born Elijah Friday — is a fast-rising Nigerian Afro-beat sensation taking the industry by storm. Hailing from the vibrant Nigerian music scene, EF Moon blends infectious melodies with raw lyrical storytelling, creating a sound that's both fresh and timeless.

With his debut album "MOON 1" and the smash single "I knoww" featuring Star Jay, EF Moon is quickly establishing himself as the voice of a new generation.
